Welcome to the World of Pet Animals

Dog

Dogs are loyal and friendly companions. They love to play and stay active, often needing exercise and attention. Dogs come in various breeds, each with its own unique personality and traits.

Cat

Cats are independent yet affectionate pets. They are curious creatures who enjoy exploring their surroundings. Known for their agility, cats often entertain with their playful antics.

Rabbit

Rabbits are gentle and social animals. They enjoy hopping around and can be kept indoors or outdoors. Rabbits are known for their soft fur and are great pets for families with children.
